Get to know our team

Mission Story

From humble beginnings, Battle Bots Gaming Studio was born out of a shared passion for creating games that bring people together. Our journey started in a small garage, where a group of friends with big dreams and boundless creativity came together to build a gaming studio that stands out. We believed that the best games are those that are easy to pick up and play, yet offer endless fun and excitement. At Battle Bots, our mission is to connect families through our unique and engaging games. We design experiences that can be enjoyed by all ages, fostering moments of joy and connection. Whether you have a few minutes or a few hours, our games are crafted to fit seamlessly into your busy life, ensuring that fun is always within reach. Join us on our adventure as we continue to create games that are not just entertaining, but also a way to bring loved ones closer together. At Battle Bots Gaming Studio, fun is our definition, and connection is our mission.

Our Values

Looking at Things from a New Perspective to Push Innovation

At Battle Bots Gaming Studio, we constantly seek out fresh perspectives and new approaches to game design. By thinking outside the box and challenging the status quo, we push the boundaries of what's possible, bringing innovative and exciting experiences to our players.

Zooming in on What Makes a Game Great

We believe that the magic of gaming lies in the details. Our team meticulously analyzes and refines every aspect of our games, ensuring that they meet the highest standards of quality. From stunning visuals to smooth gameplay, we focus on what makes games truly exceptional.

Focusing on Team Players

We design our games with community and cooperation at their core. By encouraging team play and collaboration, our games foster strong connections and create memorable experiences. We believe that playing together not only enhances the fun but also builds lasting friendships.

Solving for Fun

Creating a game that is truly fun involves understanding the intricate balance of challenge, reward, and engagement. Our team delves into the psychology of play, experimenting with different mechanics and dynamics to craft games that are captivating and enjoyable for everyone.

The Real People that Play Our Games

Inclusivity is a cornerstone of our design philosophy. We rigorously test our games with a diverse group of players to ensure they are accessible and enjoyable for all. By listening to feedback and making necessary adjustments, we strive to create games that everyone can pick up and play.
