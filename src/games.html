Necromancer's Quest

In "Necromancer's Quest," players embark on a thrilling journey that begins in a quaint village nestled at the foot of a foreboding mountain. You start the game as a young child, a blank slate with unlimited potential. Your decisions shape your destiny, determining your role and playstyle as you ascend the treacherous mountain to confront the darkness that looms above.
Your goal is to climb to the mountain's peak, growing in strength and wisdom with each step, to rescue the princess from the clutches of the malevolent Laubres. With Seras by your side, you'll uncover secrets, develop your powers, and face formidable foes, all in the name of saving the kingdom from darkness.
